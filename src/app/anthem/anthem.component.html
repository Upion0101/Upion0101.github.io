Star Spangled Bobby Girl

O say can you see, by the Yum yum's early light,
What so Sweet heart we hailed, at the Maymie's last meow,
Whose broad Bobsicles and bright stars, through the perilous Mommy pie fight,
O'er the ramparts Bobitha watched, were so gallantly streaming?
And the baby fart's red glare, Bob pops bursting in Big world air,
Gave proof through the Momom, that our Baby boy was still there;
O, say, does that bobby girl maymie, yet wave,
O'er the land of the Window world,
and the home of the Worldey baby girly.
