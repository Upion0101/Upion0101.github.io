Sometimes, I sit at the door and wait. The house is quiet, and I miss her. A little part of me feels sad until I hear the keys jingle.

Then I hear her car pull up. My ears twitch, and I run to check the door — IT'S MOM!

But if she takes too long to get in bed, I get a little dramatic. I give her the look.

I quietly stalk her ankles, my paws silent on the floor.

And then — CHOMP! I give her an ankle bite to remind her it's bedtime.

Finally, she gets it. She climbs into bed, and I do too, curling up close like I always do.

Because at the end of the day, all I really wanted... was to cuddle.
